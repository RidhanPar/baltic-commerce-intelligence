# Project Deliverables and Evaluation Rubric

## Recruiter-Facing Deliverables

1. One-page case-study README with business result and architecture.
2. Power BI executive report with a three-minute guided story.
3. Public Looker or Looker Studio exploration demonstrating governed self-service.
4. Excel finance workbook for budget, forecast, and actual-versus-plan analysis.
5. Reproducible SQL, Python, R, dbt, Snowflake, and Databricks code.
6. Executive recommendation memo and five-minute demo video.

## Power BI Pages

1. Executive Overview: revenue, margin, retention, forecast, and alerts.
2. Profitable Acquisition: CAC, 90-day LTV, ROAS, attribution, and cohorts.
3. Product Funnel: sessions to purchase, segments, and experiment results.
4. Logistics and Finance: delivery performance, cost variance, and savings.
5. Data Trust: freshness, failed tests, reconciliation, and ownership.

## What Reviewers Should See

| Capability | Proof |

| Python | Tested ingestion and validation pipeline plus analysis notebook |

## Suggested Final Recommendations

Replace these with evidence from the completed analysis:

- Shift 15% of paid-social budget from low-LTV prospecting campaigns to high-intent search and CRM reactivation.
- Raise the free-shipping threshold only for low-margin segments where experiment results show no material retention damage.
- Renegotiate or reroute underperforming logistics lanes responsible for the largest unfavorable cost variance and late deliveries.

## Scoring Rubric

- 25% decision quality and business impact
- 20% SQL and analytical depth
- 15% data model and engineering reliability
- 15% Power BI/DAX and visualization quality
- 10% experimentation and statistics
- 10% documentation and communication
- 5% Excel planning usability
